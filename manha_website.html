Smart Finance

How We Help You
Plan Your Taxes

Tax planning is not about evading taxes — it's about legally reducing your burden through smart financial decisions made throughout the year. We guide you at every step of India's financial calendar.

APRIL — START OF YEAR

Investment Declaration

Declare your investments under 80C (PPF, ELSS, LIC), 80D (health insurance), and HRA at the beginning of the financial year to lower monthly TDS from your salary.

JUNE / SEPTEMBER / DECEMBER / MARCH

Advance Tax Payments

If your tax liability exceeds ₹10,000, we calculate and help you pay advance tax in four instalments to avoid interest under Section 234B and 234C.

JANUARY — FEBRUARY

Proof Submission & Final Review

We help you submit investment proofs, reconcile Form 16, and do a final review of your taxable income to ensure there are no year-end surprises.

MARCH 31 — YEAR END

Last-Minute Tax Saving

Invest remaining limits in ELSS, make donations under 80G, or complete any outstanding filings before the financial year closes permanently.

JULY 31 — FILING DEADLINE

ITR Filing & Refund

We file your Income Tax Return accurately and on time to secure maximum refunds, avoid penalties, and keep your financial record clean for loans and visas.

Key Tax Saving Sections

Important deductions you may be missing

SECTION 80C — UP TO ₹1.5 LAKH
PPF, LIC, ELSS, home loan principal, tuition fees, NSC, 5-year FD
SECTION 80D — UP TO ₹75,000
Health insurance premiums for self, family & parents
SECTION 24 — UP TO ₹2 LAKH
Home loan interest deduction for self-occupied property
SECTION 80CCD(1B) — ₹50,000 EXTRA
Additional NPS contribution over and above 80C limit
SECTION 87A — ZERO TAX
If your total income is ≤ ₹7 lakh (new regime), you pay zero tax
